I am Maria, a pilates instructor focusing on physical therapy through fitness in Athens, Greece

After finishing my pilates certification, I spent the last decade deepening my understanding of the body, as well as the therapeutic effect of pilates on pain relief and injuries.

Being passionate about the field and the results on my clients physical strength, as well as overall wellbeing, I decided to further my studies by obtaining a Bachelor of Science degree in Physiotherapy from Queen Margaret's University of Scottland.

These days I teach personal sessions online and in person.

Group classes are also availabe at Ananda studio in Nea Smyrni, and in Happy Body physiotherapy center in Piraeus.
